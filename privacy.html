Privacy Policy

CodeVault does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Backups are created locally on the user’s Mac and saved only to folders explicitly selected by the user. The app does not upload files by itself and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

CodeVault stores app settings, selected folder permissions, and local log entries on the user’s Mac to provide its backup functionality.
